TRAINERS

The Professional Wrestling Academy is Sydney's premier professional wrestling training centre for aspiring pro wrestlers.


The PWA team provides the most experienced and superior training in the state.

Two of our home grown athletes have signed with the WWE - Billie Kay & Peyton Royce.

PWA holds training 4 days a week. We have beginner classes on Tuesdays, which are taugh by Caveman Ugg (BJ).


We have intermediate classes on Mondays and Thursdays, which are taught by Madison Eagles and Mick Moretti.


Expert Classes are on Wednesdays and are taught by Bullet Club Member Robbie Eagles (James).


Once you make it to the expert class, you will then be considered to perform at live events infront of crowds upwards of a thousand people.
